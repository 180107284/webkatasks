HOW TO MAKE UGLY SWEATER SUGAR COOKIES

  1. Make cookie dough (recipe follows) and chill.
  2. Roll out between two pieces of parchment paper to about 1/4″ thickness (a little less than that).
  3. Cut using an Ugly Sweater cutter. Do not move cookie from parchment.
  4. Remove excess dough, then slide parchment paper onto a half sheet pan.
  5. The key to nicely shaped cookies is to NOT move them after they are cut!
  6. Bake for 10-13 minutes on 350°F and let cool.
  7. Make royal icing (recipe follows) and decorate cookies. See below for tips.
  8. Ugly Sweater Christmas Cut-Out Sugar Cookies
